About Me

I am Joshua Joby, a photographer known online as Capturecrazejosh and the creative mind behind the series Unspoken Corners. Through my lens, I explore light, perspective, and atmosphere, transforming quiet everyday spaces into cinematic visual stories. My work focuses on revealing the hidden beauty and emotion within moments that often go unnoticed.
